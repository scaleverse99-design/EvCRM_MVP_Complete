# EvCRM Dealer Marketing Model — Low-Cost, High-Impact

> **Goal**: Help dealers achieve 5+ sales/month with minimal marketing spend  
> **Cost per dealer**: ₹500–2,000/month (optional, mostly free)  
> **Customer acquisition channels**: Organic, WhatsApp, Email, Social, Google, Referral  
> **Target**: 100% of dealers can implement this day 1

---

## Problem Statement

**Current state:**
- Customers discover dealerships via: foot traffic, Google Maps, signage, word-of-mouth
- Dealers have no easy way to **amplify** existing customers/inventory
- Most dealers don't do online marketing (cost, complexity, tech literacy)
- EvCRM has built the infrastructure (storefront, profile, inventory) but no **distribution** strategy

**Opportunity:**
- Leverage **existing inventory** + **existing customers** + **EvCRM platform** = low-cost, high-impact marketing

## The Marketing Model: 5 Pillars

### **Pillar 1: Subdomain Storefront as Marketing Hub** ✅

**How it creates value**:
- ✅ Professional online presence (Google-indexable)

- ✅ Test drive booking automation
- ✅ Mobile-friendly (63% of Indian traffic is mobile)
- ✅ Replaces need for dealer website (costs ₹5K–20K to build)

**Customer journey**:
```

        ↓
evcrm.in appears (marketplace)
        ↓

        ↓

        ↓
Books test drive online
```

**Marketing value**: ₹0 (included in CRM subscription)

### **Pillar 2: WhatsApp Direct Messaging** (Most Effective, ₹0)

**Why WhatsApp**:
- 500M+ WhatsApp users in India (95% smartphone penetration)
- Average dealer has 20–100 past customers
- WhatsApp has 35%+ open rate (vs email 15%)

**Dealer workflow** (5 min/day):
```
1. Open dealer dashboard → "WhatsApp Campaigns"
2. Select inventory: "New Tata Nexon EV just arrived"
3. Write message: "Hi Raj! We just got the Tata Nexon EV you asked about. ₹15.99L. Book a free test drive: ramdealers.evcrm.in"
4. Send to past customers (automated list)
5. Track clicks/conversions
```

**How to implement**:
- [ ] Add WhatsApp Business API integration to EvCRM
- [ ] Create message templates (inventory, follow-ups, offers)
- [ ] Track click-through rate (CTR) to booking
- [ ] Show ROI in dealer dashboard ("5 customers, 2 test drives, 1 booking")

**Expected results**:
- Reach: 20–50 past customers
- Click-through rate: 10–20% (industry avg: 5–10%)
- Test drive bookings: 2–4/month
- Conversions: 1–2 sales/month

**Cost**: ₹0 (Twilio WhatsApp Business API = $0.0080/message = ~₹0.66/message)
- 50 customers × 4 messages/month = 200 messages = **₹132/month** (negligible)

### **Pillar 3: Email Campaigns** (Low-cost, Automated)

**Dealer workflow**:
```
1. Dashboard → "Email Campaigns"
2. Pick template: "New Inventory", "Seasonal Offer", "Testimonial"
3. Personalize: "Hi [Name], your Ather 450X is ready for pickup"
```

- **Testimonial**: "Rahul's Ather journey: 2K km, 0 regrets"
- **Offer**: "₹50K discount on Nexon EV this month only"
- **Review request**: "Rate your test drive experience"

**Expected results**:
- Email list per dealer: 30–100 contacts
- Open rate: 15–25% (dealer brand audience)
- Click rate: 5–10% (of opens)
- Test drive bookings: 1–2/month
- Sales: 0.5–1/month from email

**Cost**: ₹0–2,000/month
- Free tier (Resend): 3,000 emails/month (covers 50 dealers × 60 emails each)
- Professional tier: ₹2,000/month (50K emails/month)

### **Pillar 5: Referral Program** (₹0–1,000, Viral Growth)

**Concept**: Incentivize existing customers to refer friends

**Workflow**:
```
Customer books test drive
        ↓
After booking confirmation:
"Share your test drive with friends → Get ₹500 discount on your next service"
        ↓
Customer shares: "Check out this EV at Ramdealers.evcrm.in"
Link includes: ?ref=customer_id
        ↓
Friend clicks → Books test drive
        ↓
Original customer gets: ₹500 discount coupon
```

**Implementation**:
- [ ] Add referral link to booking confirmation email
- [ ] Dashboard shows "Your referrals: 3 people, earned ₹1,500 credit"
- [ ] Automatic tracking (URL parameter tracking)
- [ ] Dealer can set their own incentive (₹100–1,000)

**Expected results** (per customer):
- Referral rate: 10–20% (1 in 5–10 customers refer)
- Average referrals per customer: 0.5
- Sales multiplier: +30–50% organic growth

**Cost**: ₹500–1,000/month per dealer (optional incentive budget)
